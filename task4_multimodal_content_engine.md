# Task 4: The Multimodal Content Engine

## Tool Used: Whisper AI + GPT-4o + Runway AI

## Scenario
A YouTube creator wants to turn a 10-minute video into
5 short Reels with captions, summaries, and viral headlines.
The entire process is automated using AI.

## Video Used
Title: "10 Habits That Changed My Life"
Duration: 10 minutes

## How The Workflow Works

### Step 1 — Video Transcription
Whisper AI converts audio to text with timestamps.
The full 10-minute video becomes a text transcript.

### Step 2 — Viral Segment Detection
GPT-4o prompt: "Find the 5 most engaging moments from
this transcript that are 45-60 seconds long with strong hooks."

### Step 3 — Caption and Headline Generation
AI automatically writes viral headline, Instagram caption,
and hashtags for each segment.

### Step 4 — B-roll Suggestions
AI suggests what B-roll footage to use for each moment.
Example: "show a person waking up at 5am" or
"show someone writing in a journal"

### Step 5 — Final Reels Export
Runway AI overlays B-roll on original clip, adds captions,
and exports as vertical 9:16 Reel format.

## Prompt Engineering Techniques Used
1. Multimodal input: audio and text combined
2. Viral hook identification prompt
3. Platform-specific formatting for Reels
4. B-roll description generation prompt
5. Timestamp-based segment extraction
6. Casual Urdu/Hindi tone matching
